About this path

What does a product manager do?

A product manager decides what a team builds and why. The job sits between users, business and engineering: finding the problems worth solving, framing them so a team can act, and checking whether shipped work actually moved anything.

This path covers the craft in the order you'll meet it on the job — research before prioritization, prioritization before roadmaps. Mark each topic done, learning or skipped; every topic links 2–4 vetted resources.

~3 months at 5h/week no prerequisites updated Jun 2026
